About

Who I am, what I'm into, and why I bother writing any of this down.

VX
VOID.EXE
@voidlog · Loughborough, UK

Somewhere in the English Midlands, slowly working through a backlog that grows faster than I can play it. I've been a gamer since the SNES era and a film obsessive since the first time I watched Alien and couldn't sleep for a week.

VOIDLOG is my little corner of the internet — a place to process what I've been watching, playing, reading, and listening to. I'm not a professional critic, just someone with opinions who finds that writing helps them actually remember what they thought.

Particular weaknesses: FromSoftware games, Denis Villeneuve films, sci-fi novels with ideas too big for their own good, and any TV show willing to take its time.

◈  Frequently Asked
How do you score things?
1–10, no decimals on the final score. 7 is genuinely good, not a disappointment. 10s are rare and mean something. I've never given a 1 because I stop watching things I hate.
Do you play on PC or console?
Primarily PC, but I keep a PS5 around for exclusives. I am congenitally incapable of walking past a Steam sale without buying something I won't play for two years.
What are your all-time top 3 games?
Elden Ring, Dark Souls 3, and — controversially — Outer Wilds. These are not negotiable. Ask me again in six months and they'll be the same.
How often do you post?
Irregularly, honestly. Whenever I finish something and have enough to say. No schedule, no editorial calendar — just whenever the mood takes me.
Can I recommend something to you?
Please do. I'm always looking for the next thing, especially obscure sci-fi novels and indie games that flew under the radar. The contact form below is open.
